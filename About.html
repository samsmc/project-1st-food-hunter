About

  • Why food hunter?


    From the first time I traveled abroad, in addition to discovering other cultures, I discovered a new world of gastronomy and became a foodie, so I decided to show the world the flavors that we can discover with each other and learn how to reproduce it.

    Coincidentally there's an American cooking competition television series called Food Hunters is that aired on Nickelodeon Latin America. It premiered on Sunday, October 16, 2016 in Latin America, and concluded on January 15, 2017. The project began filming in May. This program features 12 episodes of 60 minutes and is produced in collaboration with Chef Lorena Garcia and Cinemat Inc. for Nickelodeon Latin America.

    A toast to the aromas and flavors!
